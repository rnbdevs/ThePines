Places of Interest

Narayankoti Temple

Lakshmi Narayana Temple built around 09th Century. Only place in the country which has nine planetary temples in a group representing our 9 Planets.

  • More

Chopta

Known as Mini Switzerland of Uttarakhand. Famous for Camping, Rock Climbing, Trekking and as base camp for Baba Tungnath Temple.

  • More

Deoria Tal

Also known as Devariyatal - The Reflection Lake, an emerald lake perched at a height of 2,438 meters. It is a part of Kedarnath Musk Deer Sanctuary.

  • More

Kedarnath

Most remote and sacred pilgrimage among the Chota Char Dham pilgrimage. Located at an elevation of 3,583 meters near the source of Mandakini River.

  • More

Madhyamaheshwar Temple

Also known as Madmaheshwar or 2nd Kedar. Believed to be built by Pandavas, it is located at an elevation of 3,497 meters in a lush meadow, just below a high ridge.

  • More

Trans Bharat Helipad

Managed by Aryan Aviation, well-reputed name among one of the top helicopter service providers to Kedarnath from the helipad at Guptkashi.
