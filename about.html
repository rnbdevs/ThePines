About Us

Built in the heavenly hills of Uttarakhand, The Pines offers a homely feel and a charismatic experience like no one else around. The magnificient view of snow-covered mountain peaks with glittering sunrises and sunsets adds to the kind of vacation you plan for your perfect weekends. Wanting a get-away from routine life? Visit The Pines, Narayankoti, District Rudraprayag, Uttarakhand - 246439.

The Pines is a freshly constructed premium resort which has 18 double bed rooms, well suited to your weekend trip needs or a long stay in a tranquil place: Uttarakhand. With beautiful sightseeing viewpoints, you can wander around inhaling the fresh air and exhaling all your worries. If you feel like not going out, you can always stay back and enjoy the peaceful calm that our resort has to offer.

Property Highlights

  • Super Built-Up Area of 2,200 sq. meters.

  • 18 Super premium double occupancy rooms.

  • Built across 3 Floors [Step Wise construction] in the hills.

  • In-house restaurant with lip smacking traditional food.

  • On-road [Highway] property, situated right on Kedarnath highway.

  • Terrace cum viewpoint for amazing sunrise / sunset views.

  • Badminton Court available for sport geeks.

  • In-premises temple available if you want to indulge in inner peace.

  • Bon Fire area available for overnight fun gathering sessions.

  • Play cum garden area for everyone to enjoy nature walks.
